Capstone Project

KeyFit

For our Capstone Project, we explored various everyday problems that we or others experience. Through research we realized that Parkinson’s Disease and Essential Tremors is a major problem that many people experience. People who suffer from both conditions complained about having difficulties unlocking doors. After conducting extensive research, we found products that focused on different ways of unlocking doors for example, using a combination code or an app from on a smartphone. However, these products are expensive and often limited to specific door types. These products somewhat alleviate the challenges faced by individuals afflicted with Parkinson’s and Essential Tremors, but there are improvements needed. Our goal is to create a product that is cost efficient and enable the users to effortlessly insert their keys in keyholes without having concern about the shaking interfering with the key fitting or causing them to accidentally drop the keys. We used the Engineering Design Process to guide us in researching, designing and developing a prototype that is functionable.
